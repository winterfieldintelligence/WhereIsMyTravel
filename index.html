Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 17, 2026

Where Is My Travel (“we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect information when you use the Where Is My Travel app and related services.

Information We Collect

  • Approximate or precise location data: Approximate location may be requested first to provide basic map and tracking context. Precise location is requested only when needed for live tracking accuracy.
  • Background location data: Requested only when you explicitly enable “Share my live GPS (works in background)”. When enabled, sharing runs with a persistent notification.
  • Trip inputs: Start location, end location, and vehicle number (optional) to label the route.
  • Device & app data: Basic device info (model, OS version), app version, and network data needed to operate the Service.
  • Usage events: Minimal interaction data (e.g., screen opens, feature toggles) for stability and troubleshooting.

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide live vehicle tracking and route visualization.
  • Improve accuracy by smoothing multiple user locations.
  • Show nearby place names for the vehicle position.
  • Maintain app performance, security, and reliability.

Location Permission & Pop‑Up

We request location in context, only when you use a location feature (for example, tapping Start Live Tracking or Share). The app does not show a location pop‑up on app launch. If location services are OFF or required permission is missing, the pop‑up includes working buttons to turn on location or grant permission. Background location is requested only when you explicitly enable sharing.

Data Deletion

You can delete your app data any time from the in-app sidebar menu by tapping Delete My Data. This clears stored app data and removes your live location from Firebase.

Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with service providers required to run the Service:

  • Firebase (Google): Realtime Database for storing live location updates.
  • OpenStreetMap services (Nominatim/Photon): Location search and reverse‑geocoding.
  • OSRM (router.project-osrm.org): Routing paths.
  • Map tiles: OpenStreetMap tile servers via MapLibre to render maps.

Data Retention & Deletion

Live location data is stored only as long as needed to provide live tracking and recent updates. You can stop sharing at any time from within the app. You can also delete your data instantly using Delete My Data in the app menu.

Demo Mode

Demo mode uses simulated movement on your device. It does not upload location unless you enable sharing.

Your Choices

  • Turn off location sharing at any time.
  • Revoke location permissions in device settings.
  • Delete your data from the app menu.
  • Contact us to request access or additional deletion help.

Security

We use industry‑standard security measures and transport encryption (HTTPS) to protect data. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Children’s Privacy

The Service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

International Users

Your data may be processed in countries other than your own, including where our service providers operate.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Effective date” shows the last update.
